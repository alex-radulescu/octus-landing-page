Integrated

Octus is completely web-based. No need to download, install or maintain. Receive seamless updates, with no down-time. Track applicant statuses, manage candidates and manage jobs all from one space.

Intuitive

Octus adapts to your needs and assists you in getting the job done. Simple user-interface that doesn't get in the way of your progress.

Transparent
